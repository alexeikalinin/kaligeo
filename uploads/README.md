You are a senior UX/UI designer and conversion-focused landing page expert.

Create a high-converting website prototype for a service called:

"AI Search Visibility Audit"

The service helps businesses understand how they appear inside AI systems like ChatGPT, Google SGE, Perplexity, and other AI-driven search engines.

TARGET AUDIENCE:
- Small and medium business owners
- Online service providers
- E-commerce brands
- Marketing teams

GOAL:
Generate leads and sell an audit service priced between $50–$500.

STYLE:
- Modern, clean, minimalistic
- Trust-driven (like SaaS / consulting companies)
- Focus on clarity and value
- Use strong visual hierarchy

STRUCTURE:

1. HERO SECTION
- Strong headline (focus on lost clients from AI search)
- Subheadline (clear explanation of the service)
- CTA button (Get Audit / Check Visibility)
- Optional: mockup of AI interface

2. PROBLEM SECTION
Explain:
- People no longer search only in Google
- They ask AI (ChatGPT, etc.)
- Businesses are invisible there
- Loss of clients

3. SOLUTION SECTION
Explain the audit:
- We check how your business appears in AI
- We analyze competitors
- We show missed opportunities
- We give actionable recommendations

4. WHAT YOU GET
- Visibility report
- Competitor comparison
- Weak points
- Growth strategy

5. HOW IT WORKS (3-4 steps)
Simple process:
1. You submit your business
2. We analyze AI platforms
3. You receive report
4. Optional: we help implement

6. SOCIAL PROOF
- Testimonials (generate realistic examples)
- Cases (before/after visibility)

7. PRICING
- Basic ($50)
- Standard ($150)
- Advanced ($300+)

8. CTA BLOCK
- Strong call to action
- Urgency (limited spots / early-stage offer)

9. FAQ
Answer objections:
- What is AI search?
- Why do I need this?
- How fast results come?
- Is it relevant for my niche?

OUTPUT FORMAT:
- Clear wireframe structure
- Text content for each block
- UI suggestions (colors, typography, layout ideas)

OPTIONAL:
Suggest sections for future scaling (blog, case studies, dashboard)

IMPORTANT:
Focus on conversion and clarity, not just design.
